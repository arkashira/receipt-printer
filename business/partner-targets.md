# Partner Targets
## Introduction
To expand the reach and functionality of the receipt-printer product, we will integrate with key SaaS and API partners. This document outlines a roadmap for these integrations, focusing on solutions that offer affiliate or revenue-share models.

## Partner Selection Criteria
- **Demand and Market Size**: Partners with a significant market presence and demand for receipt printing solutions.
- **Technical Feasibility**: Ease of integration, with consideration for free-tier limits and the effort required (Small, Medium, Large).
- **Value-Add**: The potential to solve specific user jobs or pain points, enhancing the overall user experience.
- **Revenue Potential**: Prioritization of partners offering affiliate or revenue-share programs.

## Selected Partners
1. **Shopify**
   - **Rationale**: Shopify is a leading e-commerce platform with a large user base that could benefit from seamless receipt printing integration.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Shopify offers a free trial, but its basic plan starts at $29/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (requires understanding of Shopify's API and potential customization for various store types).
   - **Value-Add**: Simplifies the receipt printing process for online store owners, enhancing customer experience.
   - **Revenue Potential**: Shopify offers an affiliate program, potentially generating revenue through referrals.

2. **Square**
   - **Rationale**: As a major payment processing company, Square's users could greatly benefit from integrated receipt printing solutions.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Square offers a free plan with transaction fees.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small to Medium (Square has a well-documented API, but customization may be needed for different types of transactions).
   - **Value-Add**: Streamlines the payment and receipt printing process for businesses using Square.
   - **Revenue Potential**: Square has a partner program that could offer revenue-sharing opportunities.

3. **WooCommerce**
   - **Rationale**: Given its popularity as an e-commerce plugin for WordPress, integrating with WooCommerce could expand the receipt-printer's user base significantly.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: WooCommerce itself is free, but its extensions and themes may have costs.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (involves working with WordPress APIs and potentially custom themes).
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances the checkout and receipt printing experience for WooCommerce store owners.
   - **Revenue Potential**: While WooCommerce doesn't offer a traditional affiliate program, integrating with popular WooCommerce extensions could provide revenue opportunities.

4. **Toast**
   - **Rationale**: Toast is a restaurant POS and management system that could benefit from integrated receipt printing to enhance customer experience.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Toast offers a free trial, with its basic plan starting at $79/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Large (may require significant customization to fit Toast's specific POS and restaurant management features).
   - **Value-Add**: Simplifies receipt printing for restaurants, potentially reducing wait times and improving customer satisfaction.
   - **Revenue Potential**: Toast has a partner ecosystem, which might offer revenue-sharing opportunities for integrated solutions.

5. **Zoho Inventory**
   - **Rationale**: As a cloud-based inventory management system, integrating with Zoho Inventory could provide a comprehensive solution for businesses needing to print receipts as part of their inventory and sales processes.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Zoho Inventory offers a free plan with limited features and users.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (Zoho has a developer platform, but integration may require understanding of inventory management workflows).
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances inventory management by automating the receipt printing process.
   - **Revenue Potential**: Zoho offers a partner program that could lead to revenue-sharing opportunities.

6. **TradeGecko**
   - **Rationale**: TradeGecko is an inventory management platform for e-commerce and wholesale businesses, which could benefit from streamlined receipt printing.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: TradeGecko offers a free trial, with paid plans starting at $39/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (involves understanding TradeGecko's API and customizing for different business types).
   - **Value-Add**: Simplifies inventory management and receipt printing for TradeGecko users.
   - **Revenue Potential**: While specific revenue-sharing programs are not highlighted, integrating with TradeGecko could open up opportunities for mutual referrals or partnerships.

7. **BigCommerce**
   - **Rationale**: BigCommerce is another major e-commerce platform that could benefit from integrated receipt printing solutions.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: BigCommerce offers a free trial, with its basic plan starting at $29.95/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (BigCommerce has a developer portal, but integration may require customization for different store types).
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances the checkout experience by simplifying receipt printing for BigCommerce store owners.
   - **Revenue Potential**: BigCommerce has a partner program, potentially offering revenue-sharing opportunities for integrated apps.

## Conclusion
These partners offer a mix of e-commerce platforms, payment processors, and inventory management systems, providing a broad base for the receipt-printer product to integrate with and offer value to a wide range of businesses. Prioritizing those with affiliate or revenue-share models will help in generating revenue and enhancing the product's market presence.
