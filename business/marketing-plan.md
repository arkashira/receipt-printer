# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
"Streamline your receipt printing with our thermal printer abstraction layer that converts PDF receipts to printer-specific commands effortlessly."

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**
1. "Simplify your development process with our seamless PDF to thermal printer command conversion."
2. "Eliminate compatibility headaches with our universal thermal printer interface for PDF receipts."
3. "Transform the way you print receipts with our innovative thermal printer abstraction technology."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Alex the Retail Developer**  
   - **Daily Job:** Develops and maintains POS systems for retail environments.  
   - **Budget:** $5,000/month for software tools and integrations.  

2. **Persona 2: Jamie the E-commerce Manager**  
   - **Daily Job:** Manages online sales platforms and oversees order fulfillment processes.  
   - **Budget:** $3,000/month for operational software and tools.  

3. **Persona 3: Taylor the Small Business Owner**  
   - **Daily Job:** Operates a small café and handles all aspects of the business, including sales and inventory.  
   - **Budget:** $1,500/month for essential business software and hardware.

## Channels
1. **Developer Forums and Communities (e.g., Reddit, Stack Overflow)**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $50 per acquisition through targeted ads and community engagement.

2. **Content Marketing (Blog Posts, Tutorials, Webinars)**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $100 per acquisition through SEO and lead generation strategies.

3. **Partnerships with POS System Providers**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $200 per acquisition through co-marketing initiatives and bundled offerings.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1:** Launch blog post introducing the product and its benefits.
- **Week 2:** Publish a tutorial on integrating the abstraction layer with existing systems.
- **Week 3:** Host a webinar demonstrating the product in action.
- **Week 4:** Release a case study showcasing early adopters and their success stories.
- **Week 5:** Share customer testimonials and feedback on social media.
- **Week 6:** Publish an FAQ article addressing common questions and concerns.
- **Week 7:** Create a comparison chart highlighting advantages over competitors.
- **Week 8:** Release a video walkthrough of the product setup and usage.
- **Week 9:** Conduct a survey to gather user feedback and insights.
- **Week 10:** Share insights from the survey and announce product updates.
- **Week 11:** Publish a blog post on future developments and roadmap.
- **Week 12:** Recap the 90-day journey and share success metrics.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product development and begin internal testing.
- **D-0:** Official product launch with marketing materials and promotional campaigns.
- **D+30:** Analyze initial user feedback and iterate on product features.
- **D+90:** Evaluate market response, adjust marketing strategies, and set the stage for future enhancements.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** Target 1,000 DAUs by D+90.
- **$MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) Target:** Achieve $10,000 in MRR by D+90.
